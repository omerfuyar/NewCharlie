# Story of robot underground city
You are explorer that discovered a cave tunnel that leads to an ancient underground city with technology

This underground city is a home for sentient robots, they want your help to collect parts for their generator to power the whole city, they cant afford to waste power by moving so that’s why they need your help.

You collect the parts that each npc asks and you bring them to the main room where the big machine is at, when you have all the parts you can activate the machine, or destroy it if you found the secret.
