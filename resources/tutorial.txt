mapID - The unique identifier for the map. Max value is MAP_MAX_COUNT - 1.
portalTargetMapID,... - The IDs of the maps that the portals lead to, from top to bottom, left to right. Max of MAP_PORTALS_MAX_COUNT. Leave empty if there are no portals.
mapData - The data for the map, which includes the layout, interactive elements. Size must be MAP_MAX_WIDTH x MAP_MAX_HEIGHT.
